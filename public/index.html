Instructions
  1. Enter your height, your desired draw weight, and the angle you want to fire from. The first two values will be saved between runs.
  2. When you're ready, click the "Fire" button, and the simulation will begin. Inputs will be disabled while the simulation runs.
  3. As the simulation runs, you'll be able to follow your arrow's trajectory in real time, including the maximum height and distance traveled.
  4. When the simulation ends, click the "Reset" button to reset the simulation and try a different configuration. The current visualization will be lost.

Documentation
  • This app simulates an arrow fired from an Olympic recurve, allowing users to experiment with different setups without worrying about form.
  • The current version accounts for arrow mass, bow efficiency, draw length, draw weight, starting height, and vertical angle.
  • Future versions will account for air resistance (based on temperature, air pressure, and humidity), horizontal angle, and wind speed and direction.
  • I also plan to add a 3D visualization, targets, scoring, and a degree of randomness to better reflect real-world conditions.
